National Gallery Victoria
Visit to Yayoi Kusama exhibit

Location: NGV
Date: Week 4

Right after our weeks 1-3 workbook submission, we went to the Yayoi Kusuma exhibition in the National Gallery of Victoria.

Here, we were given free reign to take inspiration for Kusuma's odd yet intriguing artworks. Her works make great use of repeating patterns, especially with her iconic polka dot works.

As week 4 and onwards was going to thrust us into the world of p5.js, Kusuma's works gave us great inspiration for our beginner p5.js works as we try to recreate her works through an algorithmic process.
