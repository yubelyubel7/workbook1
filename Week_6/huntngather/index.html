Hunt and Gather

For this week, our hunt and gather theme was to search for different ways websites visualise data.

Music DNA Visualisation

pudding.cool is a cool website that visualises the way different music are related to one another. The DNA of music and how it is passed down from generations and onwards. By the end you get to see a massive family tree of how the different songs are connected.

Every Noise at Once

everynoise.com is another interesting website. It is quite similar to pudding.cool, but it takes a much more simpler approach. It simply just puts all the different music genres in your face as text and lets you scroll through the page clicking them one by one as they start playing exerpts from said genre.

1 Pixel Wealth

1 pixel wealth is another interesting visualisation of data. The site scrolls almost endlessly horizontally and really gives you the perspective of how much the wealth the richest people own in comparison to the everyday person.

Printing Money

The printing-money site from neal.fun is quite similar to the 1 Pixel Wealth website, it visualises the movement of money (income and spending) by animating the dollars moving from left to right. The more the spending/income, the faster it moves.
