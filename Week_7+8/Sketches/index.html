Project Proposal Sketches

Pencil spinner and warmer and cooler

As an artist, one of the first Chindogu sketch ideas that came to mind is to create something to fiddle and fidget with the pencil for me. So how about a machine that does all the hard pencil spinning work for me? I can't spin my pencil, so I'll let the machine do it for me!

While on the spinning, how about adding more functionality? With the constantly volatile weather and temperature, I would love to have my pencil heated or cooled to adjust to my liking, so I added in that functionality as well. The perfect luxury artist tool.

Solar-powered charging hat

This innovative hat combines fashion with function! Solar panels embedded in the brim collect energy throughout the day, which is then stored in a small battery pack hidden within the hat's design.

A USB port discreetly positioned under the brim allows you to charge your devices on the go, making this the perfect accessory for outdoor adventures and everyday urban life.

Automatic page turner

Reading a physical book while eating or doing other tasks can be challenging when you need to turn the page. This automated page turner solves that problem elegantly.

Using gentle air jets and a proximity sensor, it detects when you've reached the end of a page and automatically flips to the next one, keeping your reading experience seamless and your hands free for other activities.
